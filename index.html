selected works
Stamurai - Web Design
Designed a responsive landing page for an app named 'Stamurai' that helps people who stutter. Defined useful features with PMs and Devs. Worked across a range of disciplines including wireframing to creating pixel-perfect mockups & interactions. Boosted conversions by optimizing the UI, and achieved a 166% growth in user engagement within 3 months.
Insane Deals - Webapp
Designed an e-commerce website from scratch named 'InsaneDeals'. Built an intuitive user experience with a mobile-first mindset, while still keeping the interaction patterns consistent for desktop. Collaborated with the PM and the dev team to ideate, design, and implement new features.
Fathom Optics - Web Design
Collaborated with 3 multi-disciplinary creatives specializing in creative strategy, branding, and copywriting in a 48 hours rebranding session. We designed various deliverables for 'Fathom Optics' consisting of the copywriting strategy, brand identity, investor pitch deck, and a whole new redesigned landing page.
Mausumi - Web Design
Designed and developed an online book showcase to highlight the work of Mausumi Tarafder, a Bengali writer, with her writings modeled after her beliefs, perceptions, and words of love.
KERALAONROAD - MOBILE WEB
Redesigned and prototyped the mobile onboarding experience for 'KeralaOnRoad'. Collaborated with the Backend team to deploy new features. Optimized the UI and increased the User engagement by 35% within a month.
BUZZOMS - REBRANDING / WEB
Collaborated with 3 multi-disciplinary creatives with specialties in branding, creative strategy, and copywriting in a 48-hour rebranding session. Our deliverables for 'Buzzoms' included a copywriting strategy, brand identity, investor pitch deck, and redesigned landing page for their ecommerce platform.
KHATABOOK - STRATEGY
Planned and designed a business strategy for a few added new features on an app named 'Khatabook', a ledger / business management app for MSMEs, and implemented those design ideas into working prototypes and analyzed user feedback to assess its validity.
